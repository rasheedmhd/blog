Hi, I am

Rasheed Starlet

A Self-taught Programmer from Ghana. This is where I write about tech and my musings on life.

I usually joke that while other people learned coding because they played a game that inspired them to create their own or they wanted to build a website, I learned to code because I wanted to build an Operating System that would take Microsoft and Apple out of business. I was 17. No! I am not kidding. I am 25 now and humbled.

But why not build a toy Operating System? That is what I tried doing with Oubre OS. Thanks to Philip Opperman's awesome blog OS curriculum.
